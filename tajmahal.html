The Tajmahal 'crown of the palace',is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of the river yamuna in the indian city of agra,it was commissioned in 1632 by the mugal emperor shah jahn(reigned from 1628 to 1658) to house the tomb of his favourite wife,Mumtaz Mahal; it also house of tomb of shah jahan himself.The tomb is the centerpiece of a 17 hectare complex,which include a mousque and a guesthouse and is in formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenelated wall
